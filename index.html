About Zenith Sushi

At Zenith Sushi, we believe that great sushi is a balance of tradition, precision, and creativity. Our chefs carefully prepare every dish using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques inspired by Japanese culinary heritage. From delicate nigiri to comforting bowls of ramen, each plate is crafted to deliver a memorable dining experience.

Whether you're joining us for a relaxed meal with friends or a special evening out, Zenith Sushi offers a welcoming atmosphere where quality and flavor take center stage. We are dedicated to serving food that not only satisfies, but also celebrates the artistry of Japanese cuisine.

Our Main Dishes

KATSU

Golden, crispy cutlet with a juicy center, drizzled in rich tonkatsu sauce. Comfort on a plate.

TAKOYAKI

Bite-sized octopus balls, topped with savory sauce, mayo, and dancing bonito flakes. Irresistible street flavor.

TEMPURA

Light, airy batter coats shrimp and veggies for a golden crunch. Perfectly delicate, perfectly tasty.

GYOZA

Crispy-bottomed dumplings stuffed with seasoned meat and veggies. Dip in soy-vinegar and savor.

DORAYAKI

Fluffy pancakes hugging smooth, sweet red bean paste. A cozy Japanese treat.

TERIYAKI

Glazed chicken or beef, sweet and savory, perfectly grilled. Served with steamed rice and veggies.
